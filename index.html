Quick Tips:
  • Drag blocks from the toolbox into the canvas. Dropzones are shown as green dashed lines.
  • Double-click any block to edit it. "Text" blocks change content; "Tab" and "Return" blocks can be renamed for clarity.
  • Drag blocks into the red trash area to delete them.
  • Use presets to save and reload custom layouts.
  • For best results, disable any automatic input (like Tab or Return) on your QR scanner to avoid extra characters.
